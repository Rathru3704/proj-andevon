Our Services

We have expertise in many areas of business and management. We can help you make better decisions, offer third-party ideas, and ultimately improve your business. And we can do this all while you focus on what matters most—running your day-to-day business.

Competition Intelligence

We can help you identify exactly what sets you apart from your competitors, and what you can do to increase your market capitalization. We will identify market niches and trends you can capitalize on. If you know your enemy, you can beat them.

Customer Research

Understanding what your customers want is crucial for your business. Are they happy with your product or service? Are there specific things they wish you provided, or don't care about? We can find these answers, and help you create more loyal and profitable customers.

Team Training/Analysis

Your team is the backbone and face of your business. They are your biggest asset—and they can mean the difference between failure and success. We can help you create, expand, or improve your existing team, and ensure they are as cohesive and as productive as possible.

Temporary Placements

If you need someone now, and don't have time to recruit, we can act as an employee. Whether you need a temporary senior manager, a seasoned fixed-term project manager, technical expertise, or even some creative infusion, we can fill the gap in your business.

Marketing

Your brand, image, and presence are crucial no matter how new or well-established your business is. Are you advertising in the right places? Are your marketing campaigns successful? Are you getting the best bang for your buck? We can help you answer these, and pinpoint areas for improvement.

Coaching

If you need input for a major decision, or need to brainstorm a solution, we can be there to help. We can help offer neutral third-party ideas, free of vested interests. Sometimes all it takes is an outside perspective—look no further.

Financial Practice

We can help you improve your bottom line. We can create Business Plans, help you acquire Venture Capital funding, act as your virtual CFOs, and much more.
